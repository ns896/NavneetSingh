My Journey & Expertise

Welcome to my world of innovation and engineering excellence!

At Zendar INC., I've honed my skills in the art of designing and bringing to life mixed signal PCB boards, powering state-of-the-art automotive-grade 76-81 GHz imaging radar heads. My playground is a fusion of high-speed devices, seamlessly integrated into SOC modules using PCIE, 1000BaseT1 Ethernet, and MIPI-CSI.

During my tenure at Transonic Systems INC., I immersed myself in the fascinating realm of medical devices. My specialty? The design, debug, and meticulous failure analysis of mixed signal circuit boards. My canvas included FPGAs, CPLDs, PSOCs, and ARM MCUs, all meticulously crafted to meet the highest standards of performance and regulatory compliance.

My research journey at Adie Lab – Cornell University, where innovation knows no bounds, led me to the world of RTL design and simulation. Here, I wove cutting-edge algorithms into the fabric of Optical Coherence Tomography and Scanning Probe Microscope-based imaging systems. I even conjured up GUI-based automated diagnostics tools using the magic of LABVIEW & MATLAB, elevating optical imaging capabilities to new heights.

With a firm grip on Intel FPGAs (Quartus Prime) and Xilinx FPGAs (Vivado & Vitis), complemented by expertise in Verilog, System Verilog, Python, and embedded C, I'm a relentless explorer in search of inventive solutions to enhance biomedical devices and revolutionize healthcare outcomes.
